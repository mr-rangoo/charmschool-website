Our Departments

1. Department of Tourism and Hospitality

The Department of Tourism and Hospitality offers students the opportunity to study the dynamic fields of tourism, hotel management, and event planning. With a focus on customer service, sustainable tourism, and industry practices, the department prepares graduates for successful careers in tourism management, hotel operations, and the travel industry.

2. Department of Pure and Applied Health Sciences

The Department of Pure and Applied Health Sciences provides students with a deep understanding of health sciences, including biology, biochemistry, and medical technology. The department equips students with the knowledge and skills needed for careers in healthcare, research, and public health, focusing on both practical applications and theoretical foundations in health-related fields.

3. Department of Education

The Department of Education trains future educators with a solid foundation in teaching, learning, and curriculum development. The department focuses on providing hands-on experience in schools, research in educational methodologies, and ensuring that graduates are well-prepared to make a difference in classrooms and communities.

4. Department of Science

The Department of Science offers a broad range of programs in disciplines such as physics, chemistry, and biology. With an emphasis on practical research and experimentation, students are trained to become problem solvers and innovators in the scientific world. Graduates can pursue careers in research, development, and academia, contributing to advancements in science and technology.

5. Department of Business

The Department of Business provides students with comprehensive knowledge in business administration, economics, and management. Through specialized courses in finance, marketing, and entrepreneurship, students develop leadership skills and strategic thinking abilities to thrive in both corporate environments and the entrepreneurial world.
